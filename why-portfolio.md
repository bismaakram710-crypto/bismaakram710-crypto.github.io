<p class="wp-label">My Purpose</p>
<h1 class="wp-title">Why Portfolio?</h1>

<div class="wp-content">

<p>When Dr. Bilal Ahmad, my professor at UET Lahore, Faisalabad Campus, first suggested that we create a portfolio blog website on GitHub, I honestly did not fully understand why. But as I started working on it, I began to realize this was not just another assignment.</p>

<h2>Leaving a Digital Footprint</h2>
<p>The main objective behind this blog is to leave a digital footprint as a student of Computer Engineering. In today's world, everything is online. If you have nothing online, it is like you do not exist professionally.</p>

<h2>Benefits</h2>
<p>There are so many advantages to this practice. It builds a public record of my growth, improves my communication skills, helps other students, and builds my personal brand over time.</p>

<h2>Why GitHub?</h2>
<p>Dr. Bilal Ahmad recommended GitHub Pages because it is the platform that engineers actually use. Having a presence on GitHub shows that I am serious about the field.</p>

<h2>A Small Step With Big Impact</h2>
<p>Post by post, project by project, semester by semester — I will build something here that truly represents who I am as a student and as a future engineer.</p>

</div>
