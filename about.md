Hi, I’m Bisma Akram, a Computer Engineering student at UET Lahore, Faisalabad Campus.

Welcome to my personal portfolio blog, where I document my journey as a Computer Engineering student. This website serves as a digital record of my academic growth, technical skills, projects, assignments, and learning experiences throughout my university life.

As I progress through my degree, I aim to share the knowledge, challenges, and achievements that shape my understanding of computer engineering. From programming fundamentals and database systems to project development and problem-solving techniques, every post reflects a part of my learning journey.

Through this blog, I hope to not only track my own progress but also create a resource that may help fellow students who are learning similar concepts. I believe that documenting what we learn is one of the best ways to strengthen our understanding and build a professional digital presence.

Here, you’ll find posts about:

💻 Programming projects and coding experiences

📚 Concepts and lessons learned during my courses

📝 Assignments, challenges, and problem-solving approaches

🗄️ Database Systems and practical implementations

🚀 Personal growth, achievements, and reflections on my academic journey

Thank you for visiting my portfolio. I look forward to sharing my experiences, projects, and insights as I continue exploring the exciting world of Computer Engineering.
